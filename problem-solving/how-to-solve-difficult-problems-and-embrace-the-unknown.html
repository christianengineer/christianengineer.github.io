How to solve complex problems and embrace the unknown

Are you currently struggling with a complex problem?

My first step is to answer these questions:

Step 1: Define the Problem and Impact

  1. What does the solution look like?
  2. Where, what, or whom does this solution impact?
  3. What can you learn from the journey while arriving at this solution?

Once you answer the 3rd question, you will notice the benefits you will receive by working towards a solution. The thought of giving up has faded.

Step 2: Identify the Next Physical Action

Lets now begin:

  • Pick one of the things you listed for question 3.
  • Now answer this question: What physical action can I take to make the slightest progress?

These examples include the following physical action that can be taken toward completing it.

To Learn JavaScript

Follow these physical actions:

  • With your hand, move your mouse cursor to your preferred browser icon.
  • Press your mouse button to click and open a new browser window.
  • Place your fingers on the keyboard.
  • Type "How to print hello world in JavaScript" into the browser's search field.
  • Press the 'Enter' key with your finger to initiate the search.
  • Navigate to a Relevant Tutorial or Guide:
  • Move your mouse cursor to a link with a suitable tutorial or guide.
  • Click on the link to open the page.
  • Read the Instructions on Printing "Hello, World!" in JavaScript:
  • Move your eyes across the screen to read the instructions.
  • Follow along with any code examples or explanations provided.

To Learn DevOps

Follow these physical actions:

  • With your hand, move your mouse cursor to your preferred browser icon.
  • Press your mouse button to click and open a new browser window.
  • Place your fingers on the keyboard.
  • Type "DevOps Categories" into the search field of the browser.
  • Press the 'Enter' key with your finger to initiate the search.

Step 3: Continue Making Progress

After you complete the first physical action, repeat the same question but slightly differently:

  • What is the following physical action I can take to keep progressing?

You are now determined to solve your problem by being physically engaged in progressing toward a solution.

Your actions will inspire others through actions.

Your commitment will contribute positively to any team.
